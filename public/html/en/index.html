What is Tokyo Ruby Kaigi 10?

Held on January 13th (Sunday) and 14th (Monday/National Holiday), 2013, Tokyo Ruby Kaigi 10 will be the 10th Regional Ruby Kaigi held in the Tokyo area.

Tokyo Ruby Kaigi 10 will aims to highlight Rubyists active in the Tokyo Area. With a targetted 800 participants over two days, it will be the largest Regional Ruby Kaigi ever.

We plan to announce the exact details later.

Call for Presentations

We are now recruiting presenters for Tokyo Ruby Kaigi 10. Are you someone who has wants to present in front of Rubyists all around Tokyo? Maybe it's something you do every day, maybe it's something you're just interested in, and you've felt the topic was too lightweight for "the Ruby Kaigi". Well then, Tokyo Ruby Kaigi 10 is the place for you!

We welcome any presentations that would be of interest to Rubyists. For example, talking about other programing languages besides Ruby, or even something that doesn't relate directly to development.

The time limit for presentations is up to 15 minutes

Warning

This is not the Ruby Kaigi, and is not intended to be an international scale event. That being said, we welcome people of all backgrounds to submit a proposal.

Any costs incurred in attending the event, such as travel expenses or lodging expenses, will be the speaker's responsibility. Accepted speakers will be able to attend the event without purchasing a ticket.
